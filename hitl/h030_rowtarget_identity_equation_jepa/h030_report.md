# H030 Row-Target Identity Public-Equation HS-JEPA

## Question

H012 found a large hidden-public-state posterior. H030 asks whether the next breakthrough is not another post-hoc gate, but putting row-target identity directly into the inverse public-equation solver as a cell allowance prior.

## Falsification Target

A real identity prior should predict the H012 jackpot when H012 is held out, and should propose post-H012 movements that H024 prices below H012 under permutation stress. If it cannot do that, H012 is likely a narrow solved basin rather than a smooth route.
